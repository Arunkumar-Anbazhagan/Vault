# THE SHIVA TRILOGY: COMPLETE CHARACTER ARCHITECTURE & FAMILY TREE

## 1. THE CORE FAMILY TREE (GENEALOGY)
The lineage follows the union of the Guna tribe (Tibet) and the Royal House of Meluha.

### A. The House of Meluha (Suryavanshis)
* **Brahmanayak**: Former Emperor of Meluha.
    * **Daksha**: Current Emperor of Meluha; Son of Brahmanayak.
    * **Veerini**: Empress of Meluha; Wife of Daksha.
        * **Sati**: Eldest Daughter; A Vikarma princess and master swords woman.
            * (m. **Shiva**)
            * **Ganesh**: Sati's first-born; The Naga Lord (originally declared a stillbirth).
            * **Kartik**: Sati's second-born; A legendary warrior.
        * **Kali**: Sati's twin sister; Queen of the Nagas (born deformed).

### B. The Guna Clan & Allies
* **Manobhu**: Shiva’s maternal uncle; His primary teacher and spiritual guide.
* **Shiva**: Chief of the Gunas; The Neelkanth (Mahadev).
    * **Veerbhadra (Bhadra)**: Shiva's childhood friend and brother-figure.
        * (m. **Krittika**)

### C. The House of Ayodhya (Chandravanshis)
* **Dilipa**: Emperor of Swadweep; Ruler of Ayodhya.
    * **Bhagirath**: Prince of Ayodhya; A skilled archer and Shiva’s ally.
    * **Anandmayi**: Princess of Ayodhya; A fierce leader and strategist.
        * (m. **Parvateshwar**)

## 2. MILITARY & ADMINISTRATIVE HIERARCHY

### A. Meluhan Command (Suryavanshi)
* **Parvateshwar**: General of the Meluhan Army; A rigid follower of Law.
* **Kanakhala**: Prime Minister of Meluha; Head of Administration and Protocol.
* **Nandi**: Captain of the Meluhan Guard; Shiva’s first and most devoted follower.
* **Vidyunmali**: Brigadier; Becomes an antagonist due to religious fanaticism.
* **Drapaku**: Brigadier; A brave soldier who loses his life defending the Neelkanth.
    * **Purvaka**: Drapaku’s blind father.
* **Jhooleshwar**: Governor of Karachapa.
* **Chenardhwaj**: Governor of Maika/Lothal.

### B. Other Regional Leaders
* **Athithigva**: King of Kashi (The city of lights).
* **Chandraketu**: King of Branga.
* **Bappiraj**: Prime Minister of Branga.
* **Divodas**: Leader of the Branga community in Kashi.
* **Surapadman**: Prince of Magadh.

---

## 3. SCIENTIFIC & SPIRITUAL ORDERS

### A. The Scientists (Swan-Tribe)
* **Brahaspati**: Chief Scientist of Meluha; Shiva's best friend; Keeper of Mount Mandar.
* **Ayurvati**: Chief Physician of Meluha; The first to recognize the Neelkanth.
* **Panini**: Associate scientist working under Brahaspati.

### B. The Sages & Guardians
* **Bhrigu**: A Saptrishi Uttradhikari; The Rajguru; Primary antagonist of the third book.
    * **Tara**: A brilliant pupil of Bhrigu.
* **Gopal**: Chief of the Vasudevs (Followers of Lord Ram).
* **Mithra**: Chief of the Vayuputras (Followers of Lord Rudra).
* **Karkotak**: Prime Minister of the Naga Kingdom (Panchavati).
* **The Vasudev Pandits**: Anonymous guides located across India serving the Vishnu.

## 4. HISTORICAL, DIVINE & MINOR FIGURES

### A. The "Ancient" Legacies
* **Lord Ram**: The 7th Vishnu; Founder of the Meluhan Laws.
* **Lord Rudra**: The previous Mahadev (Destroyer of Evil).
* **Manu**: The original law-giver and founder of the Vedic way.
* **Bhoomidevi**: The spiritual matriarch of the Nagas.
* **Mohini**: A female associate of Lord Rudra.

### B. Supporting & Antagonistic Figures
* **Parshuram**: A dacoit from Branga who seeks redemption through Shiva.
* **Yakhya**: Leader of the Pakratis (Shiva's early rivals in Tibet).
* **Swuth**: Leader of the Egyptian assassins hired to target the Neelkanth.
* **Chitraangadh**: Immigration officer at Srinagar.
* **Arishtanemi**: Elite Meluhan militia guarding the Somras facility.
* **Tarak**: A resident of the port city Karachapa.
