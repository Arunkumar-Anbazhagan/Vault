Key points:

- In `adb logcat` filter specs, the level is **minimum severity**, so `MyTag:D` shows `DEBUG`, `INFO`, `WARN`, `ERROR`, `ASSERT` for `MyTag`.
    
- In code, choose the **lowest** level that correctly reflects severity: start with `D` in development, promote important events to `I`, and reserve `W`/`E`/`wtf` for genuinely abnormal or broken states.
